Core Capabilities

Everything your institution needs

HADIR consolidates WFH management, daily reporting, and admin oversight into one streamlined institutional platform.

WFH Request Management

Staff can apply for WFH dates while the system tracks weekly quota and request status, ensuring fair and structured remote work allocation.

Daily Work Log Submission

Staff can submit structured daily work logs based on approved WFH dates, providing clear documentation of remote work activity.

Location Capture

Location is recorded once during daily log submission for verification and monitoring, ensuring accountability without continuous tracking.

Admin Endorsement

Admins can review, approve, reject, endorse, and monitor staff submissions through a centralised and structured endorsement workflow.

Calendar Monitoring

A dedicated calendar view helps admins monitor who is working from home on any given day, providing clear institutional oversight.

Reports & Export

Generate meaningful WFH monitoring reports for tracking and documentation, supporting institutional compliance and transparency.

Workflow

How HADIR works

A clear, four-step process that keeps every request tracked, approved, and documented from start to finish.

01

Submit WFH Request

Staff applies for WFH date(s) through the system, specifying the required period within the weekly quota.

02

Admin Review & Approval

Admin reviews the submitted request and approves or rejects it, with status updates accessible to the staff member.

03

Daily Log Submission

On the approved WFH day, staff submits a structured daily work log with location capture for verification.

04

Endorsement & Monitoring

Admin endorses the daily log and monitors all WFH activity through calendar views and downloadable reports.

Security & Privacy

Built with privacy and trust in mind

HADIR is designed to protect staff privacy while meeting the monitoring requirements of the institution.

Institutional Google Authentication

HADIR uses institutional Google account access. Only registered users from the institution can sign in and use the system.

One-Time Location Capture

Location is recorded once during daily log submission only. The system does not continuously or passively track staff location.

Restricted Access Control

Access to HADIR is restricted to registered users only. Unauthorised individuals cannot access any staff data or records.

No Continuous Surveillance

HADIR is a reporting tool, not a surveillance system. Staff are monitored through structured submissions, not passive data collection.
